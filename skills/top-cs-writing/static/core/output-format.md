# Writing output format

Return:

1. `Route`
2. `Central argument`
3. `Claim-evidence-boundary table`
4. `Argument graph`
5. `Rhetorical moves and topic-sentence scaffold` for long sections
6. `Outline and paragraph-job map`
7. `Terminology ledger` for multi-paragraph or multi-section work
8. `English draft`
9. `Artifact and appendix plan`, including figure briefs when handoff is active
10. `Citation verification state` when citation checking is active
11. `Missing inputs`

For a small, fully specified passage, compress planning items but retain evidence checks. Keep diagnostics in Chinese and manuscript prose in English unless requested otherwise.
