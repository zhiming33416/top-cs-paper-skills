# Venue response protocols

Last verified: 2026-07-03.

- **WWW 2026:** authors receive a rebuttal period; the process is non-interactive after submission. Do not promise later dialogue. Source: https://www2026.thewebconf.org/calls/research-tracks.html
- **ICLR 2026:** OpenReview supports public or restricted author comments and paper revisions during discussion; communicate changes clearly, and do not assume reviewers will inspect every revision. Source: https://iclr.cc/Conferences/2026/AuthorGuide
- **ICML 2026:** prepare a response to each official review, followed by limited reviewer-author follow-up. Preserve anonymity and current character limits supplied by the live form rather than guessing. A revised paper cannot be uploaded during author feedback, so unverified edits remain planned. Source: https://icml.cc/Conferences/2026/AuthorInstructions
- **Generic:** do not assert word limits, interaction rules, or permitted revisions. Request the current official instructions.
