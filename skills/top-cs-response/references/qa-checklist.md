# Response QA

## Coverage

- Every editor and reviewer requirement has a stable ID, response, and status.
- Compound comments are split without losing original wording.
- Duplicate and conflicting concerns are linked but remain individually traceable.

## Factuality

- Every result, statistic, experiment, citation, and manuscript location is supplied and verified.
- Numbers, software versions, dataset IDs, figure/table labels, and claim boundaries are preserved.
- Completed and planned work are never conflated.
- Missing facts remain `AUTHOR_INPUT_NEEDED` or visible placeholders.

## Scientific adequacy

- Direct answer addresses the actual concern rather than only revising prose.
- Action is proportional to severity and affected claim.
- Unsupported causality, novelty, generality, significance, and efficiency are narrowed.
- Unavailable evidence leaves an explicit limitation.

## Tone

- Response is direct, professional, and evidence-forward.
- Disagreement restates the concern fairly and gives evidence or a scope boundary.
- No reviewer accusation, invented identity, excessive gratitude, or convenience-based refusal remains.

## Traceability

- Every claimed change has a location or placeholder.
- Revised excerpts match the supplied revision.
- Cross-round IDs and unresolved states remain stable.
- The revision ledger agrees with the letter and manuscript diff.

## Protocol and integrity

- Venue/year/phase permissions and live budgets are verified or marked unknown.
- Conference and journal workflows are not mixed.
- Anonymity and confidential content are preserved.
- Ethics, compliance, or data-integrity concerns are not cosmetically rewritten.

## Package usability

- Required files are present and named correctly.
- LaTeX placeholders remain visible until resolved and templates compile when verification is claimed.
- Chinese confirmation contains only actionable missing facts and decisions.
- Readiness state matches unresolved risks.
