# Revision verification

When the author claims a change, require one of:

- a supplied revised passage with a stable location;
- a submission/revision diff showing the change;
- a verified manuscript version containing it.

For each ledger row record `issue ID`, `claimed action`, `old location`, `new location`, `diff evidence`, and `status`. Use `verified` only when the artifact verifies the change. Otherwise use `evidence-needed`, `planned`, `cannot-complete`, or `author-input-needed` and preserve `[LOCATION NEEDED]`.

Check that a revision does not contradict another response, silently strengthen a claim, remove negative evidence, break anonymity, or exceed the venue's permitted revision scope.
