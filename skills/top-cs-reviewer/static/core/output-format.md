# Reviewer output format

Return:

1. `Review setup and assessment boundary`
2. `Neutral contribution summary`
3. `Major strengths`
4. `Technical audit`
5. `Evidence and reproducibility audit`
6. `Venue, scope, readability, and compliance audit`
7. `Citation and figure integrity audit` when activated
8. `Anchored issue ledger`
9. `Prioritized decision risks`
10. `Decision-relevant questions`
11. `Revision plan`
12. `Not-assessable items and confidence limits`

Each major issue must follow `references/issue-contract.md`. Do not emit an accept/reject score unless explicitly requested and supported by the selected venue's current form.
