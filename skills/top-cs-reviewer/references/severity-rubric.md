# Severity rubric

- `blocking`: invalidates a central claim, violates a desk-reject rule, or prevents meaningful assessment.
- `major`: materially weakens contribution, soundness, evidence, reproducibility, or venue fit and needs substantive revision.
- `minor`: localized clarity, reporting, or presentation defect with a clear fix.
- `question`: uncertainty whose answer may change severity or interpretation.
- `not assessable`: required artifact or information was not provided.

Do not inflate preferences into major issues.
