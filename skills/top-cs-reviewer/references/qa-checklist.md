# Reviewer QA

- Summary is neutral and recognizable.
- Every criticism points to a claim, location, artifact, or explicitly missing item.
- Severity matches consequence for the paper's central case.
- Venue requirements come from the selected verified profile.
- Questions are decision-relevant and not disguised demands for unrelated work.
- Strengths and negative evidence are both represented.
- No reviewer identity, hidden knowledge, experiment, citation, or editorial decision is invented.
