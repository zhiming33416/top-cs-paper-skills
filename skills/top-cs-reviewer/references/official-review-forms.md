# Official review-form boundaries

Last verified from local 2026 official snapshots: 2026-07-05.

- **ICLR:** use the official reviewer guide for the current form and update the recommendation after discussion or verified revisions. Do not import historical rating scales.
- **ICML:** assess soundness, presentation, significance, and originality separately. Ask only questions whose answers could change the evaluation. Account for the author response in the final justification. For an actual review, confirm the reviewer-specific LLM policy before using AI assistance (`official-icml2026-assigned-llm-review-policy`).
- **WWW Research:** the local corpus contains author/rebuttal guidance but no verified public reviewer form. Audit author-visible scope, Web relevance, evidence, and compliance; label reviewer-form fields `unsupported` rather than borrowing ICLR or ICML dimensions.
- **Generic:** provide an evidence audit without venue scores or process claims.

Appendix and supplement reading is not guaranteed where official author instructions say it is optional. Flag central dependencies instead of assuming reviewers will inspect them.
