# Language and Chinese-to-English workflow

Default to Chinese diagnostics and English manuscript text.

When source notes are Chinese or mixed-language:

1. Recover the intended claim and logical relation before translating.
2. Rebuild paragraph order around topic, evidence, interpretation, and boundary.
3. Use established technical terms consistently; maintain a terminology ledger for ambiguous translations.
4. Prefer concrete subjects and active verbs. Avoid long nominal chains and empty openings.
5. Preserve hedging, negation, quantities, and comparison direction exactly.

Avoid generic AI prose such as *in today's rapidly evolving landscape*, *plays a pivotal role*, *remarkable*, and repeated *it is worth noting*. Do not imitate memorable phrasing from source papers.
