# Reader workflow

Organize the paper so a time-constrained reviewer can answer these questions in order:

1. **Relevance:** What problem is addressed, and why does it matter for this venue or community?
2. **Distinction:** What precise capability, result, or understanding differs from the closest work?
3. **Trust:** Which evidence establishes each important claim under which assumptions?
4. **Use:** Can a reader reproduce, compare, or apply the contribution?
5. **Boundary:** Where does the result stop generalizing, and what remains uncertain?

Use this sequence as a diagnostic, not a mandatory paragraph template. Do not bury relevance behind implementation detail, state distinction without evidence, or defer decisive boundaries until optional material.
