# Figure-to-claim handoff

Plan and audit figures in the writing, polishing, reviewer, and response skills; render and export figures only in `top-cs-figure`.

For every required figure, create a record conforming to `contracts/figure-brief.schema.yaml`. Bind it to at least one claim and one research question. Specify the evidence source, panel jobs, required encodings, expected observation, caption draft, manuscript callout, and evidence status.

Use `planned`, `missing`, or `unverified` when data or the artifact is unavailable. Expected observations are design targets, not completed findings. A caption may describe protocol and visible encodings, but must not claim a result that the supplied artifact or data does not establish.

Handoff boundary:

- Writing creates the brief and prose callout.
- Polishing checks caption, terminology, labels, and claim alignment.
- Reviewer audits evidential sufficiency and overstatement.
- Response records requested changes and verification status.
- `top-cs-figure` owns rendering backend, visual grammar, palette, typography, export, and image QA.
