# Source basis

Use `../_shared/rule-provenance.yaml` to interpret labels.

- Venue limits and format constraints are `official-policy`.
- Promoted abstract, contribution, related-work, and section-placement patterns are `corpus-derived` from the 90-paper structural corpus.
- All preserved quantities, citations, comparisons, uncertainty, and claim boundaries are `author-artifact` facts.
- The immutable-fact pass, terminology ledger, revision modes, style diagnostics, phrase families, before/after examples, and LaTeX repair order are `conservative-implementation` rules.
- Output headings and the revision ledger schema are `output-contract` rules.
- Every example is `synthetic-example` unless a source ID is explicitly attached.

Do not market general editing advice as an observed venue preference. Do not infer that a wording choice improves acceptance. Corpus-derived patterns may guide structure but never justify copying distinctive phrasing.
