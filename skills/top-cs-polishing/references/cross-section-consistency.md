# Cross-section consistency

Assign stable IDs to major claims and technical terms. Compare their scope, polarity, quantities, comparator, and uncertainty across sections. Flag:

- title or abstract claims broader than experiments;
- conclusion claims not present in the evidence ledger;
- terms or symbols introduced under multiple names;
- citations moved away from the proposition they support;
- limitations that conflict with earlier universal wording;
- planned changes described as completed.

Resolve by narrowing wording or requesting evidence, not by harmonizing every section to the strongest version.
