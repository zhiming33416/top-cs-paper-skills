# Polishing failure modes

- Surface polish that leaves a missing premise or unsupported leap.
- Promotional substitutions such as `shows` for `suggests` or `general` for a tested regime.
- Citation drift after sentence reordering.
- Lost negation, comparison direction, units, or scope during Chinese-to-English revision.
- Terminology variation that makes one component appear to be several.
- Long nominal chains, empty transitions, generic AI openings, and repeated significance claims.
- Broken LaTeX commands, labels, math delimiters, comments, or bibliography keys.

Report any risk that cannot be resolved without author input.
