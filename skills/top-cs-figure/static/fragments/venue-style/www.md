# Venue Figure Style: WWW Research

Evidence status comes from `visual-style-rules.yaml`. At the 2026-07-13 snapshot WWW Research has 30 verified independent sources and reports `style_status: promoted`, `anchor_status: usable`, and `profile_status: usable`. These remain separate states.

- Use a venue-derived profile only when both `style_status: promoted` and `profile_status: usable`; otherwise use the complete generic semantic system.
- Treat observed blue/red/violet/green families as anchors, not as a ready-made palette. Ordered, continuous, and dark-overlay tokens are accessibility-constrained constructions.
- Report the live eligible count, profile-support count, stability, provenance tier, and fallback reason; corpus accents are defaults, never official WWW colors.
- Keep Web relevance visible in caption/callout when the figure supports a Web-specific claim.
- Separate system, user, graph, platform, or Web-scale dimensions visually when they are central to the paper claim.
