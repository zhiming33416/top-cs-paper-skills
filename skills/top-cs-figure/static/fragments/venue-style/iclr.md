# Venue Figure Style: ICLR

Evidence status comes from `visual-style-rules.yaml`. Use a derived profile only when both `style_status: promoted` and `profile_status: usable`; otherwise retain generic semantic roles and report the evidence gap.

- Prefer readable comparison and trend panels over dense decorative composites.
- Use standard categorical contrast carefully; many ICLR figures tolerate stronger accents, but semantic consistency across panels matters more than hue count.
- State metric direction and uncertainty definitions near the panel or in the caption.
- Treat embeddings and attention-like visualizations as explanatory unless paired with supplied quantitative evidence.
- At the 2026-07-13 snapshot 30 verified independent sources support usable anchors and profiles. Blue/orange/green/violet are observed anchor families; ramps, neutral centers, and dark-overlay tokens are constructed. Retain marker/line/hatch redundancy and re-read the rules file rather than assuming this state remains current.
