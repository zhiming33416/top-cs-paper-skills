# Ranking and Critical Difference

Use for externally computed average ranks across declared datasets or tasks. Require method and rank columns.

- Lower rank is normally better; state direction explicitly.
- Keep method labels separated above and below the rank axis.
- Draw a critical-difference span only from a supplied value.
- Do not compute tests or significance in the renderer.
- Do not equate neighboring ranks with statistical equivalence without the declared procedure.

Caption obligations include ranking unit, tie handling, test procedure, adjustment, and supplied critical difference. QA checks rank bounds, label overlap, and explicit statistical provenance.
