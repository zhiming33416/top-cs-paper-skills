# Tradeoff Frontier

Use when readers must choose among operating points under two competing metrics. Require x, y, and both metric directions.

- Plot every supplied operating point before drawing a frontier.
- Connect only non-dominated supplied points; do not smooth or extrapolate.
- Label important points or method groups without hiding dominated cases.
- Keep hardware, budget, and operating conditions in provenance.
- Use log axes only with positive data and explicit rationale.

Caption obligations define both directions, operating-point generation, and system conditions. QA checks direction metadata, log domains, label overlap, and that a frontier does not imply unsupported interpolation.
