- Author-provided data, manuscript text, figure briefs, captions, scripts, and existing figure files control scientific facts.
- Shared venue files provide conservative venue context, not proof of current formatting compliance. Verify current official instructions before final submission.

- Visual-style files under `evidence/derived/visual-style-*` are corpus-derived aggregate evidence. They store no raw figures, source data, page images, or caption text and must carry promotion/preliminary/gap status.
- The `nature-figure` project is workflow inspiration for the short-router/static-fragment/reference structure and figure-contract habit only.
- Do not copy external wording, code, palettes, templates, image assets, venue rules, or claims about acceptance benefit from external projects.
