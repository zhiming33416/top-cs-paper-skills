# Caption And Callout Alignment

Check the figure as part of the manuscript argument.

- Caption title should name the figure's job, not restate the visual type.
- Every panel label in the caption must exist in the figure, and every figure panel should be explained.
- The manuscript callout should state the claim supported by the figure, not only "see Fig. N".
- Use the same dataset, metric, model, method, and module names in figure, caption, and manuscript.
- Keep evidence status honest: planned, partial, unverified, or available.
- Do not claim a result in the caption that is absent from supplied data or the rendered figure.

When updating a figure brief, preserve `figure_id`, `claim_ids`, and `research_question` unless the manuscript argument changed.
